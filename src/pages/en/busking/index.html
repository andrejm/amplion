You too can come and make the streets of Banská Štiavnica sing and vibrate at the international festival of new cabaret and street art AMPLIFIER.

 

A festival of street theatre without street artists of different genres? Unacceptable! That is why we invite buskers of all countries, all you street musicians and singers, beatboxers, dancers, actors, painters, fire-tamers and other original streetsters. Add the charming Banská Štiavnica, branded by UNESCO to your list of performances for this year. Help us make the streets of this medieval town sing and make all the viewers of the 5th edition of the annual international Festival AMPLIFIER – New cabaret & street art smile.

What awaits you?

The AMPLIFIER Festival is the right place for your street show!

You can send your application and a link to a video of your performance until June 30th, 2020. The buskers who will be part of the festival lineup will be notified by the festival directors on July 15th, 2020.

NOTE: Only previously announced and invited buskers may perform as part of the AMPLIFIER festival, taking place September 25 th – September 27th, 2020.

Deadline for applications: June 30th, 2020
