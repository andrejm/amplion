What is AMPLIFIER?

  • 3 days and 2 nights full of theatre, cabaret, concerts, street performances and unique happenings for everyone with a bit of youth in their heart. Children’s programme included!
  • Performances in historical houses of Štiavnica or non-traditional exteriors, bands performing on the trotuár promenade, buskers in crooked alleyways under the azure sky. Art mixed with a sprinkling of paradise.
  • Events aimed at the development of a civil society and engaged art. Special programmes created only for this festival commenting on recent events.
